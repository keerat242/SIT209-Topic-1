WHAT IS IOT?


Iot is Internet of things which analyse large amount of data by collecting it from the environment using different sensors and then perform specific tasks related to the data generated. It leads to :-
  • convenient lifestyle
  • reducing labour
  • automation
  • work smarter
  • saves cost

ACTIVITY 2

WHAT IS SMART HOME?

a smart home/building means having a centralized control of energy consumption, lightening, security system and their automation and the effective consumption of electricity and other resources. This can have:-
  • smart energy systems
  • air monitoring systems
  • gas leakage
  • temperature control
  • motion detectors
